Senior Machine Learning Engineer

About the Role:
We are looking for a Senior Machine Learning Engineer to join our AI team. You will design, build, and deploy production-grade ML models that power our core products.

Responsibilities:
- Design and implement scalable ML pipelines for real-time inference
- Lead model development from research to production deployment
- Mentor junior engineers and conduct code reviews
- Collaborate with product and data teams to define ML solutions
- Optimize model performance, latency, and cost efficiency
- Build and maintain monitoring and alerting for ML systems

Required Skills:
- Python (expert level)
- TensorFlow or PyTorch
- Machine Learning and Deep Learning
- MLOps (model deployment, monitoring, CI/CD)
- SQL and data processing (Pandas, NumPy)
- Docker and Kubernetes
- Cloud platforms (AWS or GCP)

Preferred Skills:
- NLP or Computer Vision experience
- Spark or distributed computing
- Leadership and mentoring experience
- FastAPI or Flask for model serving
- Airflow for workflow orchestration

Experience:
- 5-10 years of professional software engineering experience
- At least 3 years focused on Machine Learning

Education:
- Master's or Ph.D. in Computer Science, Machine Learning, or related field

Soft Skills:
- Strong communication and presentation skills
- Leadership and team collaboration
- Problem solving and critical thinking
- Adaptability and willingness to learn new technologies
