Senior Machine Learning Engineer

Company: TechVision AI
Location: Bangalore, India (Hybrid)
Department: AI & Machine Learning

About the Role:
We are looking for a Senior Machine Learning Engineer to join our growing AI team. You will design, develop, and deploy production-grade machine learning models that power our core products. This role requires deep expertise in ML, strong software engineering skills, and the ability to lead technical initiatives.

Responsibilities:
- Design and implement end-to-end ML pipelines from data preprocessing to model deployment
- Build and optimize deep learning models for NLP, computer vision, or recommendation systems
- Deploy models to production using containerized microservices architecture
- Implement MLOps best practices including CI/CD for ML, model monitoring, and A/B testing
- Collaborate with product managers and stakeholders to translate business requirements into ML solutions
- Mentor junior engineers and conduct code reviews
- Stay current with latest ML research and evaluate new techniques for production applicability
- Contribute to the development of our internal ML platform and tooling

Required Skills:
- Python (expert level)
- TensorFlow or PyTorch (production experience required)
- Scikit-learn and traditional ML algorithms
- MLOps tools (MLflow, Airflow, or Kubeflow)
- Docker and Kubernetes for model serving
- SQL and data processing (Pandas, NumPy)
- AWS or GCP cloud services
- Git and CI/CD practices
- REST API development (FastAPI or Flask)
- Linux command line proficiency

Preferred Skills:
- NLP or Computer Vision specialization
- Spark or distributed computing
- Terraform for infrastructure management
- Experience with LLMs and Generative AI
- Hugging Face Transformers
- Model optimization (quantization, distillation, pruning)
- Technical blog writing or conference presentations

Experience:
- 5-10 years of professional experience in software engineering or ML
- At least 3 years of hands-on ML/AI experience
- Experience deploying ML models to production at scale

Education:
- Master's or Ph.D. in Computer Science, Machine Learning, Statistics, or related field
- Bachelor's degree with exceptional experience will also be considered

Soft Skills:
- Strong communication and presentation skills
- Leadership and mentoring ability
- Problem-solving mindset
- Ability to work in cross-functional teams
- Adaptability to rapidly changing requirements

What We Offer:
- Competitive salary and equity
- Flexible hybrid work arrangement
- Annual conference budget
- Learning and development stipend
- Health and wellness benefits
